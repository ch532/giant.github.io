About Connect Gold

Our Mission

To connect individuals with valuable content, opportunities, brands, and digital tools that simplify their lives and drive engagement across Nigeria and beyond.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted and engaging platform for trending content and effective advertising.

Our Story

Connect Gold started with a simple idea — provide quality content to readers while offering a platform where brands and individuals can showcase what they offer. From viral campus gist and digital tools to social trends and insightful guides, we bring value to both readers and advertisers.

Why We Exist

Our main goal is to deliver content that informs, entertains, and guides, while providing powerful ad spaces and partnership opportunities to businesses who want to reach Nigeria’s digital audience.

About the Founder – Okoye Chibuike

Okoye Chibuike, born on 2nd June 1996, is from Anambra State, Nigeria. He is the visionary behind Connect Gold. From an early age,Okoye Chibuike had a deep passion for knowledge and technology. He began his journey in the world of digital marketing through online advertisements on social media. Over time, he realized the need to own a personal advertising platform that goes beyond traditional methods. This led to the creation of Connect Gold — a platform that connects companies and brands with customers and buyers through relevant content and impactful advertising. His vision continues to shape the way brands engage with audiences online.
