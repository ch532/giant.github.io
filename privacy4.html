Privacy Policy for Protomax

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

Protomax ("we," "our," or "us") provides a multi-tool utility experience. This policy details how we handle data related to our AI, scanning, and environmental awareness features.

1. Information We Collect

  • Environmental & System Awareness: To provide the "Awareness Snapshot," the app accesses real-time data from your device sensors and system state, including:
    • Device State: Battery level, Wi-Fi status, Network type (Cellular/Data), and Headset connectivity.
    • Contextual Environment: Local weather conditions, temperature, and wind speed.
    • Nearby Devices: Detection of nearby Bluetooth (BLE) beacons.
  • Camera Access: Used exclusively for "Smart Scan" features (barcode reading, document scanning, and face detection). Images are processed locally or ephemerally.
  • Precise Location: With your consent, we access GPS coordinates to provide accurate weather data and mapping services.
  • Generative AI Data: Text, voice, or files uploaded to "Ask AI" are processed to provide responses. We do not use this content for training proprietary models.
  • Storage: Access is limited to files you explicitly choose to upload for scanning or AI analysis.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display your current environment and device status in the "Awareness Snapshot."
  • To provide AI-generated insights and scanning capabilities.
  • To facilitate location-based searches via the Map interface.
  • To manage in-app advertisements and premium "Ad-Free" subscriptions.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize the following services to power app functionality:

  • Google Play Services & Firebase: For core operations, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • Google Maps / Mapbox: For mapping and location data.
  • Google ML Kit: For on-device machine learning and image recognition.
  • Ad Networks (LevelPlay, Start.io, Vungle): For displaying sponsored content.

4. Data Retention & User Control

Awareness data (like battery or weather) is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers. You can manage, limit, or revoke permissions for Location, Camera, and Nearby Devices at any time through your Android System Settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

Protomax is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If such data is discovered, it is deleted immediately.

6. Security

We employ standard technical safeguards to protect your data. However, no internet-based service can be guaranteed 100% secure.
