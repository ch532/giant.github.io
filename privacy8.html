Java ADK Assistant Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Java ADK Assistant handles your information. We prioritize your privacy and the security of your development environment.

Privacy Assurance: Your credentials are used only to authenticate with Google's AI services. No user data is collected, stored on our servers, or shared with third parties.

1. Data Security & Storage

  • Local Authentication: API keys provided by the user are stored locally within the IDE's secure storage system. These keys are never transmitted to any servers owned by the developer.
  • Data Transmission: All requests sent from the plugin to Google AI services are encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS (SSL/TLS) protocols.
  • No Telemetry: We do not track your usage habits, code snippets, or project structures.

2. Third-Party Services

Java ADK Assistant interfaces with Google Gemini/Vertex AI. Use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not provide your data to any other third parties.
